Introduction

With My world - My Hearing Suite, manual data entry for the audiologists and paper-based surveys for the patients are a thing of the past.

The patients can easily access the application to do surveys daily. The audiologists can see instant results from the patients by tracking real-world data instead of in-clinic testing results, and to create the survey simply from the existed question pool.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the advantage of this application compared with others?

Our Application mainly focused on the hearing area. Therefore, we can record the GPS and photo in the survey which can give the audiologist better understanding of patient current situation.
Moreover, this application will plan to generate printed form after audiologist creat a suvery which can be useful for patient who not have mobile phone.

I am an audiologist. How do I get started?

Register an account on the web portal: http://deco3801-unicorn.uqcloud.net
To set up the first survey template, create a new survey from scratch, add all your survey questions, then save it as a template.
To send a survey, firstly add new clients on the Clients page. After creating a new survey from a template or from scratch, select the clients who should do this survey.

How do I get access to my patient-side app?

A patient account will be registered by your audiologist and you can receive an email containing the account details including a default password. Log into your account on the mobile app, go to the setting screen and change your password. You can access the survey once it is set by your audiologist.
